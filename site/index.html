Frequently Asked Questions

Is AllFreeAlerts really free?

Yes, completely free. We do not charge for access, require signups, or hide listings behind a paywall. Our site is supported by advertising.

How often do you update listings?

Every day. We check dozens of trusted sources each morning for the latest sweepstakes, freebies, and settlement claims.

Are these sweepstakes legitimate?

Yes — every listing links directly to the official entry page or claim form. We recommend always reading the official rules before entering or providing personal information.

How do class-action settlements work?

When companies lose or settle lawsuits, affected consumers can file claims for refunds. Some require proof of purchase, while others pay everyone who files. We clearly mark which is which.

No. You can browse, search, and access all listings without any account. Your saved items are stored locally in your browser for convenience.
