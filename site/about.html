How It Works

Every morning, our automated system checks over 20 trusted sources for new sweepstakes, freebies, and settlement claims. New listings go through a quality check to verify the link works, the offer is legitimate, and the entry goes directly to the official source.

Every night, a separate system rechecks all existing listings. Expired offers, dead links, and completed giveaways are automatically removed. This means the listings you see on AllFreeAlerts are always current and active.

The result: a clean, organized, up-to-date directory of free stuff that you can trust — updated twice a day, every day.

Our Editorial Process

Every listing on AllFreeAlerts goes through a multi-step verification process before it appears on the site:

Step 1: Discovery. Our system checks over 20 trusted sources every morning, including federal settlement registries, brand websites, verified contest platforms, and community forums where real users share legitimate offers.

Step 2: Quality check. New listings are automatically validated: we verify the link works, confirm it goes directly to the official source (not a middleman or affiliate), check that the offer is genuinely free, and filter out anything misleading — like items disguised as freebies that actually require a purchase.

Step 3: Daily re-verification. Every night, our system rechecks every existing listing. Dead links, expired offers, and completed giveaways are automatically removed. Settlement deadlines are monitored so nothing stays listed past its claim window.

Step 4: Manual review. Entries that pass automated checks but raise flags — unusual patterns, borderline eligibility requirements, or unfamiliar sources — are reviewed by our team before going live.

This process means the listings you see are current, active, and link directly to official sources. If you ever find a listing that seems wrong, email us and we will investigate immediately.
